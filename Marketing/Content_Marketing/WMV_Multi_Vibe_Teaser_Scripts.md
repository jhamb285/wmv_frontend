## Production Notes

### Transition Library
Ensure your editor has these transitions ready:
- **Whip pans** - horizontal camera moves
- **Smash cuts** - hard cuts on beat
- **Zoom pushes** - forward camera movement
- **Glitch cuts** - digital distortion
- **Clock wipes** - circular reveal
- **Morph dissolves** - one image morphing to another
- **Particle bursts** - elements exploding outward

### FREE Event Badge Specifications
- **Color:** #4CAF50 (green)
- **Size:** Small badge overlay in corner of scene
- **Font:** Bold, DM Sans
- **Placement:** Bottom right or top left of frame
- **Frequency:** Appear on 2-3 scenes per video to establish pattern

### Color Consistency by Vibe
Each vibe should have a subtle color grade:
- **Electronic:** Purple (#5234A9)
- **Live Music:** Teal (#7DC7D6)
- **Sports:** Orange (#FF7A3D)
- **Brunch:** Gold
- **Beach/Yoga:** Green (FREE association)
- **Comedy:** Warm amber
- **Arabic Music:** Gold/traditional
- **Bollywood:** Pink/gold

### Sound Design Tips
- Add subtle whoosh sounds on transitions
- Use beat drops for scene changes
- Include ambient sound beds (crowd noise, music snippets) very low in mix
- Sync visual cuts perfectly to music beats

### Loop Optimization
End each video in a way that visually connects back to the beginning:
- **Script #1:** End frame of pins could transition back to calendar
- **Script #2:** Final brand could spin back into slot machine
- **Script #3:** Clock could restart
- **Script #4:** Brand could morph back into mood faces
- **Script #5:** Equation could reset to question marks
