## Executive Summary

**Product:** Where's My Vibe - AI-powered event finder for Dubai  
**Target Audience:** Young professionals (25-35), Gen Z (18-24), and tourists exploring Dubai  
**Platform Focus:** Instagram Reels & Stories exclusively  
**Production Approach:** AI-generated video content + professional human editing  
**Launch Market:** Dubai (Web app V1, mobile apps to follow)

**Campaign Tone:**
- Mix of playful and sophisticated (video-dependent)
- Light and funny (mostly), sometimes aspirational
- Relatable and authentic, not overly casual

**Campaign Objectives:**
- Build anticipation through mystery-driven teasers
- Showcase the problem-solution journey authentically
- Create FOMO-driven viral content
- Position as Dubai's first AI-powered event discovery platform showing ALL events
- Drive Instagram follows and early adopter sign-ups

## Core Messaging Framework

### The Problem (Pain Points to Highlight)

1. **App Overload Fatigue**
   - "Scrolling through 10+ different apps just to find plans"
   - Instagram, Eventbrite, Facebook, What's On Dubai, Time Out, Platinumlist
   - Information overload with conflicting details

2. **Missing Out on Hidden Gems**
   - "That epic event you didn't know existed until it was over"
   - Events buried in Instagram stories that disappear
   - No centralized source for ALL events

3. **The Vibe Uncertainty Problem**
   - "Is this place more hip-hop or house?"
   - "Will this brunch be chill or party vibes?"
   - "Is this comedy show cringe or actual comedy?"
   - Committing money and time without knowing the actual atmosphere

4. **Dubai-Specific Pain Points**
   - Events spread across Marina, Downtown, JBR, DIFC, Al Quoz
   - No idea what's happening where on any given night
   - Special offers (1+1 brunches, ladies' nights) buried in fine print
   - Mix of free community events and ticketed shows - impossible to track

### The Solution (USPs)

**Primary Taglines:**
- "Find Your Vibe in Dubai"
- "Every Event. One Map. Your Vibe."
- "Dubai's First AI-Powered Event Finder"

**KEY DIFFERENTIATOR:**
Unlike Time Out Dubai, What's On, and Platinumlist (which focus primarily on paid/ticketed events), **Where's My Vibe shows EVERYTHING** - from free community gatherings and pop-ups to ticketed concerts and exclusive club nights. We're the complete picture, not just the commercial one.

**Key Features to Showcase:**
1. **All events on ONE interactive map** (no more app-hopping)
2. **Every type of event** (FREE, ticketed, AND paid - not just commercial listings)
3. **Vibe-categorized** (Music, Nightlife, Sports, Food, Comedy)
4. **Color-coded markers** for instant visual recognition
5. **Sub-categories** (Electronic, Hip-Hop, Arabic, Bollywood music, etc.)
6. **Complete info** (timing, tickets, prices, special offers)
7. **Direct links** to venue Instagram pages
8. **AI-powered** discovery (first-of-its-kind positioning)

## Production Specifications

### AI Video Generation Tools Recommended

1. **Primary Tool: Runway Gen-3**
   - Best for: Realistic Dubai scenes, lifestyle shots
   - Strengths: High-quality output, consistent generation

2. **Secondary Tool: Pika Labs**
   - Best for: Quick UI mockups, animations, transitions
   - Strengths: Fast generation, good for text animations

3. **Supplementary: Midjourney (for stills)**
   - Best for: Creating base images for animation
   - Use for establishing shots, interface designs

4. **Editing: CapCut Pro or Similar**
   - For: Text overlays, transitions, final editing
   - Essential for brand consistency
   - Free tier available

### Video Specifications

**Instagram Reels Format:**
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- Duration: Teasers 7-12s / Trailers 30-60s
- Frame Rate: 30fps minimum
- File Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Max File Size: 4GB

**Quality Requirements:**
- Resolution: 1080x1920px minimum
- Bitrate: 8-12 Mbps
- Audio: 44.1kHz, stereo

### Brand Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary Purple: #5234A9 (rgb: 82, 52, 169)
- Secondary Purple: #400AD4 (rgb: 64, 10, 212)
- Accent Teal: #7DC7D6 (rgb: 125, 199, 214)
- Accent Orange: #FF7A3D (for Food category)
- Background Dark: #1B1B1B
- FREE Event Badge: #4CAF50 (green)
- PAID Event Badge: #FFB300 (gold)

**Typography:**
- Primary: DM Sans / Inter (sans-serif)
- Headings: Bold, 72-96px
- Body: Regular, 36-48px
- Captions: Always include for accessibility

**Logo Usage:**
- Include logo in last 2 seconds of every video
- Minimum size: 200x200px
- Always on contrasting background

### Music & Audio

**Music Sources:**
- **Primary:** Instagram Reels audio library (royalty-free)
- **Secondary:** Pixabay Audio Library (free, no attribution required)

- **Voice:** Female, warm, professional but approachable

- **Delivery:** Conversational, not overly formal
- **Pacing:** Clear articulation, natural pauses

### Text Overlay Best Practices

**Hierarchy:**
- Hook text: First 1 second, large, bold
- Supporting text: 2-3 words max per frame
- CTA: Last frame, centered, with emoji

**Readability:**
- White text on dark background (or vice versa)
- Minimum font size: 36px
- Add subtle drop shadow or stroke for contrast
- Never place text over busy backgrounds
- Use color coding: GREEN for FREE, GOLD for PAID badges

**Animation:**
- Appear: 0.2s fade or slide in
- Display: Minimum 1.5s per text frame
- Disappear: 0.2s fade out
- Use smooth easing (ease-in-out)
